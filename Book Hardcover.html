BASICS TO BOOK HARDCOVER

MATERIALS REQUIRED:

  1. Bone Folder
  2. Book
  3. Scissors
  4. Metal Ruler
  5. Glue
  6. Cardboard
  7. Brush
  8. Book Press
  9. Cover Paper

PROCEDURE:

  1. Cut two pieces of cardboard or chipboard to the desired size for the front and back covers.
  2. Cut a piece of book cloth or fabric slightly larger than the cardboard. Apply glue to the cardboard and carefully position the fabric. Smooth out any wrinkles and trim the excess fabric.
  3. Apply a thin layer of glue to the spine of the book block and the inside of the covers.
  4. Align the book block between the covers, ensuring the endpapers are properly positioned.
  5. Clamp or weight the book to allow the glue to dry completely.
